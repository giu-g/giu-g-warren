What (risks I take in my writing)?

Choosing ideas that seem random at first, but are actually very specific and connected to my own experiences, pushes me to be more creative in my writing, which is a major goal for me.

Why (it’s risky)?

This approach can be challenging because it can go terribly wrong if it is not done carefully. For example, when I chose Brazilian folklore as a topic, it was something my readers did not necessarily identify with, which can create a gap in shared context. If that gap is not managed well, the connection I am trying to make may not come across clearly.

Why (I do it anyway)?

It makes the work more interesting for me, because it motivates me to research more deeply and form connections I probably would not have made otherwise.
